Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee beverage brewed by forcing a small amount of nearly boiling water through finely-ground coffee beans. It is known for its intense and robust flavor, providing a quick and powerful caffeine kick.

Price: $3.99

Americano

Americano is a popular espresso-based coffee drink made by diluting espresso with hot water. It has a similar strength to traditional brewed coffee but with a distinct flavor profile.

Price: $3.99

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is a classic Italian coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It is known for its creamy texture and balanced flavors.

Price: $4.99

Latte

Latte is a popular coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. It has a smooth and creamy texture, often topped with a layer of milk foam and sometimes flavored with syrups.

Price: $4.99
